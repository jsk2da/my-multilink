Calm from the start

"We play in the yard first — until the energy runs out.
Then we begin."

Before any grooming, every dog gets time outside to run, sniff, and settle in. Once they've burned off that energy and started to feel comfortable around me, we move inside — and the whole session tends to go much more smoothly.

It's just the two of us. One dog at a time, in a private space — no unfamiliar sounds, no strange smells from other animals. Sue is with your dog from start to finish.

Good to know

Cancellations & Rescheduling

You're welcome to cancel or reschedule up to 48 hours before your appointment at no charge. Cancellations after that window, or no-shows, will forfeit the deposit. You can manage your booking directly from the confirmation email.

Matting

Honest coat descriptions help — both for your dog and for accurate pricing. If I find significant matting on the day, the session may take longer, or I may recommend a shorter cut for your dog's comfort. If you're unsure, feel free to send a photo beforehand and I'll let you know what to expect.

Aggression

If your dog has a history of biting or reactive behaviour, please let me know when you book. I'd rather have that conversation upfront than be caught off guard — it's better for both of us, and especially for your dog. Severe cases may not be a good fit, but let's talk first.
